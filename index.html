Frequently asked questions

A few things people often ask.

How do I pay?

Cash on collection/delivery, or bank transfer — whichever's easiest for you. Payment details can be arranged when we confirm your order.

What if my order doesn't quite fit the bag sizes?

No problem — just let us know roughly what you're bringing and we'll weigh it and give you an accurate price, or suggest the best option for what you need.

How do I book a collection or drop-off?

Message us on WhatsApp, Facebook, or email — whichever you prefer — with what you need done, and we'll arrange a time that works for you.

Do you offer same-day service?

It depends on how busy we are — get in touch and ask, and we'll always tell you honestly what's possible.
